Event Details

Experience the thrill of our annual 4x4x48 Challenge – a 48‑hour endurance run where participants complete 4 miles every 4 hours. This year, the event kicks off at 6 PM on April 4th and concludes with the final leg at 2 PM on Sunday, April 6th.

All legs start and finish in front of Mackal Gym, following a scenic bike path. Whether you're an avid runner or simply looking to support a worthy cause, there's a place for you in this event!

About Doctors Without Borders

Doctors Without Borders (Médecins Sans Frontières) is an international humanitarian organization dedicated to providing medical care where it’s needed most – especially in areas affected by conflict, disasters, or epidemics. Their tireless work saves lives and alleviates suffering worldwide.

Support their mission by participating in the challenge and/or donating to this incredible cause.
