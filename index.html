RULES AND SETUP

Everyone will be assigned a team of 3 randomly.

Every 48 hours, 5 new tasks will drop at 7am with varied point values based on difficulty (200, 400, 600, 800, or 1000).

Each team will have the 48 hours to do the tasks, and provide evidence (photo/video emailed to scaregames24@gmail.com ).

There will be one 500 point task in each task drop, that only one team can claim (whoever does it first).

The normal tasks can be completed by multiple teams each drop.
