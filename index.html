RULES AND SETUP

Everyone will be assigned a team of 3 randomly.

Every 48 hours, 5 new tasks will drop at 7am with varied point values based on difficulty (200, 400, 600, 800, or 1000).

Each team will have the 48 hours to do the tasks, and provide evidence (photo/video uploaded to This Google Drive Folder ).

There will be one 500 point task in each task drop, that only 3 teams can claim (first team gets 500, 2nd gets 250, 3rd gets 125).

The normal tasks can be completed by multiple teams each drop.

EXTRA TASK!: Doing this task at any point during the 2 weeks will give 2000 points : Walk up to a random group of at least 5 strangers, introduce yourself, and piss your pants in front of them.
